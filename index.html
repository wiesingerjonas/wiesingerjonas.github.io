About

Hi, my name is Jonas Wiesinger and I am currently studying at the HTL Leonding.
I am (very much) into filmmaking and photography and I want to make it my profession.

Filmmaking

I have experience in lots of different fields like:

  • wedding filmography
  • music videos
  • after movies
  • taping concerts
  • advertisement
  • documentations
  • livestreams

I liked working on those projects and I can imagine working on similar ones again, but what I enjoy most is creating short films.


Personal Preferences

I love the creative process of turning a text-based script into a form of visual storytelling.

I love analysing movies, sometimes breaking them down frame by frame, studying and trying to understand why the Director of Photography shot certain scenes the way he did.

Photography

Another big hobby of mine is photography. In the last years I oftentimes got the opportunity to photograph at concerts, weddings, for advertises and so on.

Moreover I am known for my very fast editing workflow. I oftentimes send my edited images the same day to my clients.
